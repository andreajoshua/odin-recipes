Goat Cheese Salad

This goat cheese salad is delicious, easy to make, and healthy.

Additionally, it takes under 10 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • goat cheese
  • arugula
  • almonds
  • tofu
  • grapes
  • balsamic

Steps

  1. Cook the tofu to get it crispy
  2. Roast the almonds then chop them
  3. Halve the grapes
  4. Combine ingredients together
  5. sprinkle balsamic
